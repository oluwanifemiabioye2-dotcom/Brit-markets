Step 1 of 6

Who is a Ghostwriter?

A ghostwriter is a professional writer who creates content that is officially credited to another person. You write, they take the credit, you get paid.

Why Become a Ghostwriter?

  • Write books, articles, speeches, and content for paying clients
  • Get paid directly for your writing skills and creativity
  • Choose to work anonymously or negotiate writing credit
  • Build a diverse portfolio across multiple industries
  • Set your own schedule and work from anywhere
Getting Started: After this course, you'll access your dashboard where the AI will ask what type of writing you want to do — daily chapters, full books, blogs, scripts, tweets, or all of them!

Your AI Writing Assistant

Every writer on Brit Market gets a personal AI assistant in their dashboard. Think of it as your writing coach, editor, and designer — all in one.

What the AI Can Do For You

  • 3-Day Training: Learn story structure, character development, dialogue, and pacing
  • Cover Design: Get suggestions for eye-catching story covers and layouts
  • Grammar Check: Instantly correct spelling mistakes and grammar errors
  • Writing Tips: Receive suggestions to improve tension, flow, and engagement
  • Genre Guidance: Get help choosing between dark romance, sci-fi, supernatural, etc.
In a hurry? You can skip the AI training completely and start writing immediately. The AI will still be there to help whenever you need it.
